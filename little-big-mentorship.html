Little Big Mentorship Program

1-2 semester-long game dev mentorship

About the Program

The Little Big Mentorship Program connects aspiring designers, developers, artists, and sound designers with experienced peers and industry professionals. Over the course of the semester, matches work together to build skills, refine portfolios, and navigate the gaming landscape.
