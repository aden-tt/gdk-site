Little Big Mentorship Program

1-2 semester-long game dev mentorship

About the Program

The Little Big Mentorship Program connects aspiring designers, developers, artists, and sound designers with experienced peers and industry professionals. Over the course of 1-2 semesters, matches work together to build skills, refine portfolios, and navigate the gaming landscape.
