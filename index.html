WELCOME TO GDK!

University of Central Florida's official game development club

GameDev Knights (GDK) is UCF's official game development club. We are a community of students passionate about creating games. Whether you want to sharpen your technical skills, build a portfolio to break into the industry, or just meet people who share your interests, you’re welcome here.

We host regular meetings at the downtown campus, alongside hands-on workshops, game jams, team projects, mentorship programs, and networking events. No matter your major or experience level, there’s a place for you to learn, grow, and make friends.

JOIN THE COMMUNITY! ⚔️

GDK is open to creators of all skill levels, backgrounds, and majors. If you love games and want to meet people who make them, you're in the right place. Check out our Get Involved page to learn how to become a member, or just join our Discord community and say hi!
