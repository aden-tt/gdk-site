WELCOME TO GDK!

University of Central Florida's official game development club

GameDev Knights (GDK) is UCF's official game development club. Our Discord community has over 1200 active members. We have moderators able to provide solutions to your Game Dev problems!

Our club is open to students of all skill levels interested in game development. Dues-paying members get exclusive access to perks, tools, our vault, showcase participation, and more!

Ready to level up?

Whether you're a seasoned dev or just picking up your first engine, there's a place for you in GDK. Join our Discord, pay dues, and unlock the full experience.

WHAT WE DO

Meetings

Socials

Kick back, play some games, and hang out with an awesome community of students who share your passion for game creation.

Workshops

Level up your programming, game design, and digital art skills with hands-on, student-led technical tutorials.

Game Jams

Race against the clock to design, develop, and polish unique games alongside fellow club members during weekend-long marathons.

Team Projects

Collaborate in multi-disciplinary teams over the semester to build larger, portfolio-ready game projects from scratch.

Mentorship

Get paired up with experienced upperclassmen or club veterans who will guide your portfolio growth, project workflow, and skills.

GDC Trip

Join club cohorts traveling to the Game Developers Conference to network, learn, and experience the industry on a massive scale.

Career Fair

Connect directly with local studios, recruiters, and alumni looking for fresh talent at our student-focused networking events.
