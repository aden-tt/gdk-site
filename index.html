WELCOME TO GDK!

University of Central Florida's official game development club

GameDev Knights (GDK) is UCF's official game development club. Our Discord community has over 1200 active members. We have moderators able to provide solutions to your Game Dev problems!

Our club is open to students of all skill levels interested in game development. Dues-paying members get exclusive access to perks, tools, our vault, showcase participation, and more!

Ready to level up?

Whether you're a seasoned dev or just picking up your first engine, there's a place for you in GDK. Join our Discord, pay dues, and unlock the full experience.
