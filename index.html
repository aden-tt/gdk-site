WELCOME TO GDK!

University of Central Florida's official game development club

GameDev Knights (GDK) is UCF's official game development club. We are a community of students passionate about creating games. We host regular meetings at the downtown campus, alongside hands-on workshops, game jams, team projects, mentorship programs, and networking events.

Whether you want to sharpen your technical skills, build a portfolio to break into the industry, or just meet people who share your interests, you’re welcome here.
