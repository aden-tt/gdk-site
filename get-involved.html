GET INVOLVED

Ways to connect with UCF's game dev community

01

JOIN OUR DISCORD

Join our server to stay in the loop! It’s the easiest way to meet other students, share what you're working on, ask for advice, and get regular updates about club events.

ENTER SERVER
02

REGISTER ON KNIGHTCONNECT

Official UCF student organization tracking. Joining our roster here ensures you are recognized as an official club entity for university administration and event attendance.

JOIN ROSTER
03

FOLLOW OUR SOCIALS

Want to see what we're up to? Follow our socials to get the latest info on our workshops, networking opportunities, speaker events, and club announcements. Connect with us on Instagram and LinkedIn to stay tuned for upcoming events and industry opportunities.

04

BECOME A PAID MEMBER

Becoming a paid member is NEVER required to be a part of our community.

All students are welcome to attend our meetings, participate in community game jams, the project program, and hang out in the Discord — free of charge.

... however, being a paid member does come with some cool perks! Mainly, access to our mentorship program and being eligible to go to GDC with us in the spring. By supporting the club, you give us the ability to run bigger and better events and programs for our community. Did we mention official GDK swag?
