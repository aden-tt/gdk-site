BECOME A PAID MEMBER

Paid membership is completely optional. All students are welcome to attend our general body meetings, participate in community game jams, join the project program, and hang out in the Discord server free of charge.

If you choose to support the club financially, dues go directly toward funding larger community events, operational costs, and food for meetings. In return, paid members receive a few additional benefits, including access to our mentorship program, eligibility for the annual spring GDC trip, and official club merchandise.
