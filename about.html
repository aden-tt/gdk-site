ABOUT US!

Our Mission

Founded to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world game production, GameDev Knights (GDK) provides a collaborative environment for developers at the University of Central Florida. We bring together programmers, digital artists, sound designers, writers, and designers to build functional, interactive portfolios from the ground up.

Join the Production Pipeline

You don't need a portfolio to get started. GDK operates on an open-learning model where industry tools, peer mentorship, and cross-discipline teams are accessible to anyone with a passion for game creation.

Club History & Growth

What started as a small meetup of passion-driven developers has grown into an organization with over 1,200 Discord community members. Over the years, GDK projects have gone on to win student awards, launch on commercial storefronts, and serve as the foundation for our members landing positions at major AAA and indie studios worldwide.

Our Team

Behind GameDev Knights is a dedicated group of student creators, developers, and organizers. We work together to build workshops, host events, and support everyone in our growing game dev community.
